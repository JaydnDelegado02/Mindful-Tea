Tea and Mindfulness Workshops

Introducing Tea and Mindfulness Workshops

We are pleased to offer a series of Tea and Mindfulness Workshops for those looking to explore this ancient practice in a modern way. With tea being one of the oldest beverages in the world, it has deeproots in all cultures around the globe. Our Tea and Mindfulness Workshops will provide an expertly guided, insightful, and interactive journey into the many health benefits of tea consumption and its connection to mindfulness.

We are now taking registrations for our next Tea and Mindfulness Workshops beginning in April and running through the summer months. The workshops are held on Saturdays in our studio and are two hours in length. Spots are limited so please register early.

What You Will Learn

The content of our Tea and Mindfulness Workshops is unique and comprehensive. You will learn about the history of tea and its various global origins. We will also explore the many health benefits associated with different types of teas, and how to select and prepare teas for maximum health benefit. Additionally, we will be covering the basics of mindfulness and how tea consumption can be a gateway to this powerful practice.

At the end of the Tea and Mindfulness Workshops, you will have a foundational knowledge of tea and its many health benefits, as well as the skills necessary to develop a mindful practice. You will also receive a complimentary sample bundle of premium teas to help begin your journey into the world of tea.

Registration and Fees

The fee for our Tea and Mindfulness Workshops is $50 per person, with all materials included. Registration can be completed online or in person at the studio. If you have any questions, please feel free to call us at the number provided.

We look forward to having you join us for this unique Tea and Mindfulness Workshops experience!
